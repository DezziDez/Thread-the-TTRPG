[[Core Rules/Chapter 5 ~ Items/Items\|Items]] are objects that [[Core Rules/Chapter 3 ~ Characters/Character\|Characters]] can [[Core Rules/Chapter 7 ~ Events/Actions\|use]], [[Core Rules/Chapter 5 ~ Items/Inventory and Carry Capacity/Equipped\|equip]], and [[Core Rules/Chapter 5 ~ Items/Inventory and Carry Capacity/Accessories\|store]]. All [[Core Rules/Chapter 5 ~ Items/Items\|Items]] can be broken down into a list of [[Core Rules/Chapter 5 ~ Items/Properties\|Properties]] that described how the [[Core Rules/Chapter 5 ~ Items/Items\|Item]] functions. 

The number of properties an [[Core Rules/Chapter 5 ~ Items/Items\|Item]] can have is determined by the [[Core Rules/Chapter 3 ~ Characters/Ranks\|Rank]] of the [[Core Rules/Chapter 5 ~ Items/Items\|Item]]. An [[Core Rules/Chapter 5 ~ Items/Items\|Item]] has a number of properties equal to the [[Core Rules/Chapter 3 ~ Characters/Ranks\|Rank]] of the [[Core Rules/Chapter 5 ~ Items/Items\|Item]].

[[Core Rules/Chapter 5 ~ Items/Items\|Items]] can also be [[Core Rules/Chapter 5 ~ Items/Crafting and Materials/Crafting\|crafted]] while a [[Core Rules/Chapter 3 ~ Characters/Character\|Character]] is [[Core Rules/Chapter 4 ~ Mechanics/Resting\|Resting]] to make unique [[Core Rules/Chapter 5 ~ Items/Items\|Items]] or to gain other resources.
