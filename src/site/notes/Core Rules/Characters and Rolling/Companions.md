[[Core Rules/Characters and Rolling/Character\|Characters]] can have any number of companions or familiars at their command or control. From animals, strange creatures, or even people.

If a [[Core Rules/Characters and Rolling/Character\|Character]] is under the control or command of another character, that character's initiative is always the same as the controlling [[Core Rules/Characters and Rolling/Character\|Characters]] unless otherwise stated.

Begging of turn effects trigger at the same time as well as end of turn effects. The controlling [[Core Rules/Characters and Rolling/Character\|Character]] can choose which [[Core Rules/Actions and Turn Time/Actions\|Actions]] from which [[Core Rules/Characters and Rolling/Character\|Characters]] happen in which order. Allowing for direct coordination and teamwork.
